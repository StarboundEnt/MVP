# Go / No-Go Review Plan

## Objective
Define the process for the final launch readiness decision once all checklist items are complete.

## Timeline
- **T-72 hours**: Confirm checklist status; circulate agenda and latest build notes.
- **T-48 hours**: Hold go/no-go meeting (Engineering, Product, Compliance, Operations, Support). Capture decisions + action items.
- **T-24 hours**: Final verification of deployments, monitoring, app store approvals.
- **T-0**: Launch execution window with on-call staff present.

## Agenda (T-48h Meeting)
1. Technical readiness (backend, mobile apps, monitoring).
2. Data governance & legal sign-off.
3. Operational support (on-call, runbooks, comms).
4. Risk review & mitigation plan.
5. Decision + assigned owners for remaining tasks.

## Scheduling
- Use shared calendar invite titled "Starbound MVP Go/No-Go" with attendance required.
- Attach filled `docs/mvp_launch_checklist.md`, `ops/monitoring_setup.md`, and deployment plan deck.
- Record minutes and publish to project channel within 2 hours.

## Post-Decision Actions
- **Go**: Trigger deployment playbook, send stakeholder comms, start enhanced monitoring.
- **No-Go**: Document blockers, update launch date, assign owners, schedule follow-up review.
